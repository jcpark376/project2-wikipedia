The Slovakia national under-18 and under-19 basketball team is the national junior basketball team that represents Slovakia in international under-18 and under-19 tournaments.[1] They are controlled by the Slovak Basketball Association. The team competes at the FIBA U18 European Championship, with the opportunity to qualify for the FIBA Under-19 World Cup.[2][3]
