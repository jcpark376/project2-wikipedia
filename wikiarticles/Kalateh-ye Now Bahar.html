Kalateh-ye Now Bahar (Persian: كلاته نوبهار‎, also Romanized as Kalāteh-ye Now Bahār)[1] is a village in Shamkan Rural District, Sheshtomad District, Sabzevar County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 779, in 198 families.[2]
