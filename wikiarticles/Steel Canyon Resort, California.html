Steel Canyon Resort is an unincorporated community in Napa County, California.[1] It lies at an elevation of 443 feet (135 m).[1] Steel Canyon Resort is at the mouth of Steel Canyon, 11 miles (17.7 km) south of Berryessa Peak.[2]

In August 2020, parts of Steel Canyon Resort was evacuated due to the Hennessey Fire, which resulted in the burning of over 315,000 acres (127,476 ha) in five counties, including in Steel Canyon.[3]
