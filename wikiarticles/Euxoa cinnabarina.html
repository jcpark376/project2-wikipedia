Euxoa cinnabarina is a species of cutworm or dart moth in the family Noctuidae first described by William Barnes and James Halliday McDunnough in 1918.[1][2][3][4] It is found in North America.[1]

The MONA or Hodges number for Euxoa cinnabarina is 10797.[5]
