Oxalicibacterium faecigallinarum is a Gram-negative, rod-shaped, non-spore-forming, yellow-pigmented, oxidase and catalase positive,[3] and oxalotrophic bacterium from the genus Oxalicibacterium and family Oxalobacteraceae.[4]
