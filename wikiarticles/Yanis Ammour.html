Yanis Ammour (born 21 May 1999) is a French professional footballer who plays as a forward for AS Béziers, on loan from Montpellier.

Professional career[edit]

On 4 April 2018, Ammour signed his first professional contract with his childhood club Montpellier HSC.[1] He made his professional debut in a 2–0 Ligue 1 win over Girondins de Bordeaux on 21 October 2018.[2]

Personal life[edit]

Born in France, Ammour is of Tunisian descent.[3]
