Magreanops is a phacopid trilobite in the family Phacopidae. The type species is Magreanops renateae[1] and the genus also includes Magreanops monachus,[1] both described from the Frasnian-aged Bovesse Formation.[1] and Moulin Liénaux Formations in Belgium during 2018.
