ishop TV is an Australian free-to-air television channel that was launched on 30 April 2013. The channel is owned by Prime Media Group and Brand Developers, broadcasting infomercials and home shopping.

When sister channel 4ME on channel 64 was closed after its operator Brand New Media went into administration,[1] channel 64 was re-purposed as a feed of ishop TV. The extra feed was later removed in June 2016.
