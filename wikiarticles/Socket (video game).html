Socket, known in Japan as Time Dominator 1st (タイムドミネーター1ｓｔ), is a platform game developed and published by Vic Tokai for the Mega Drive/Genesis.

Reception[edit]

The game has been criticized for too closely resembling Sonic the Hedgehog.[1]
