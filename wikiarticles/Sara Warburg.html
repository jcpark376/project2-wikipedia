Sara Warburg (1805–1884), was a German banker.[1]

Life[edit]

Her father was German banker Moses Marcus Warburg, who found in Hamburg with his brother Gerson Warburg the bank M.M.Warburg & CO in 1798.[2] She was the manager of the M.M.Warburg & CO, the 4th–biggest bank in Hamburg, after the death of her spouse in 1856 until 1865. In 1829, she married her cousin Samuel Warburg (1798–1856), with whom she had four children: Marianne (1830–1882), Rosa (1833–1908), Moritz and Siegmund.
