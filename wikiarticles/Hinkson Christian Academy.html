Hinkson Christian Academy (HCA; Russian: Школа им. Хинксона) is a K-12 Christian international school in the South-Western Administrative Okrug, Moscow, Russia.[1]

Campus Crusade for Christ, Int. established the school in 1991. In the 2012–2013 school year it had 165 students. The Association of Christian Schools International accredits the school.[2]
