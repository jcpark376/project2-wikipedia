Shurik (Persian: شوريك‎, also Romanized as Shūrīk and Shoorik; also known as Shorik)[1] is a village in Koreh Soni Rural District, in the Central District of Salmas County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,182, in 222 families.[2]
