Mai Yoshida (吉田 舞, Yoshida Mai, born May 12, 1981 in Hakodate, Hokkaido, Japan)[1] is a Japanese short animation artist. Yoshida is based in London, and showcases her work internationally at art exhibitions and film festivals. She moved to London to study art in 2000. After she completed Illustration BA at Camberwell College of Art (one of University of Art London), she started her MA in Communication Art & Design at Royal College of Art and graduated in 2006.
