Dasmurt (Persian: داسمورت‎, also Romanized as Dāsmūrt)[1] is a village in Ahmadfedaleh Rural District, Sardasht District, Dezful County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 53, in 6 families.[2]
