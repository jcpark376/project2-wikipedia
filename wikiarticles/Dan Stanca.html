Dan Stanca

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Dan Stanca (Bucharest, September 30, 1955) is a Romanian writer.[1]

Life[edit]

He studied English literature at the University of Bucharest and worked as a journalist for the cultural magazine România Liberă until 2008.

Prizes[edit]

  • Uniunii Scriitorilor din România, 2015, „Ghetsimani ’51” (Editura Cartea Românească). [2]
