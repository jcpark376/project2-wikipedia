The Creanga Mare (also: Craca Mare) is a right tributary of the river Târnava Mică in Romania.[1][2] It flows into the Târnava Mică in Bucin. Its length is 12 km (7.5 mi) and its basin size is 34 km2 (13 sq mi).[2]
