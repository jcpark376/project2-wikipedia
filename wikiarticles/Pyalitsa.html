Pyalitsa (Russian: Пялица) is a rural locality (a selo) in Tersky District of Murmansk Oblast, Russia,[1] located on the Kola Peninsula at a height of 1 meter (3 ft 3 in) above sea level. Population: 14 (2010 Census).[2]
