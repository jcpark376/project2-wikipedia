List of governors of the Banque Centrale du Congo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Wikipedia list article

This is a list of the governors of the Banque Centrale du Congo in Kinshasa. The bank, which is the country's central bank, was founded after independence in 1960.

  • Albert Ndele, 1961–1970[1]
  • Jules Fontaine Sambwa, 1970–1977[1]
  • Charles Bofossa Wambea Nkosso, 1977–1979[1]
  • Jules Croy Emony Mondanga, 1979–1980[1]
  • Jules Fontaine Sambwa, 1980–1985 (second term)[1]
  • Pierre Pay-Pay wa Syakasighe, 1985–1991[1]
  • Jean Nyembo Shabani, 1991–1993[1]
  • Joseph Buhendwa bwa Mushasa, 1993–1994[1]
  • Godefroid Ndiang Kabul, 1994[1]
  • Djamboleka Lona Okitongono, 1995–1997[1]
  • Jean-Claude Masangu Mulongo, 1997–14.05.2013[1]
  • Deogratias Mutombo Mwana Nyembo, 14.05.2013–present
