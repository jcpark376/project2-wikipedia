Sharifabad (Persian: شريف اباد‎, also Romanized as Sharīfābād; also known as Sharīfābād-e Pā’īn)[1] is a village in Hoseynabad Rural District, in the Central District of Anar County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 21, in 5 families.[2]
