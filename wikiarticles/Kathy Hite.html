Kathy Hite-James (born September 8, 1948) is an American professional golfer who played on the LPGA Tour.

Hite won once on the LPGA Tour[1] in 1981.[2]

Hite-James was inducted into the South Carolina Golf Hall of Fame in 2000[3] and the Winthrop Athletics Hall of Fame in 2007.[4] She now lives in Palm Desert, California.[4]
