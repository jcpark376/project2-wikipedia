The 49th annual Miss Puerto Rico Universe competition was held in the fall of 2003 in Puerto Rico. Alba Reyes won the pageant[1] and represented Puerto Rico at Miss Universe 2004 in Quito, Ecuador.[2]

Notes[edit]

  • Miss Cidra, Alba Reyes, represented Puerto Rico at Miss Universe 2004 in Quito, Ecuador where she finished as 2nd runner-up and also won Miss Photogenic. This was Puerto Rico's 4th consecutive Miss Photogenic win.
  • Miss Carolina, Shanira Blanco, is now a well-respected and successful TV show host in the island. She is currently hosting the TV Show Flashazo VIP through the Univision Puerto Rico Network. She also represented Puerto Rico at Miss Earth 2004 in Diliman, Quezon City, Philippines and is the current national director for Miss Mundo de Puerto Rico.
  • Miss Fajardo, Cassandra Castro, later became Miss World Puerto Rico 2004, Cassandra placed as one of the Top 10 finalists in the Top Model fast-track competition at Miss World 2004, but failed to place as a finalist during the final night.[3] Cassandra represented Puerto Rico at Miss Tourism Queen International 2008 where she placed as a finalist and finished in 6th place overall and obtained the title of Continental Queen of Americas.[4]
  • Miss Villalba, Paulette Arias, is a well known model in the island where she won the prestigious "La Cara de Imagen L'Oreal".
