Eero Elo (born April 26, 1990) is a Finnish professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing for the SCL Tigers of the Swiss National League (NL). Elo was selected by the Minnesota Wild in the 5th round (145th overall) of the 2008 NHL Entry Draft.

Elo made his SM-liiga debut playing with Lukko during the 2009–10 SM-liiga season.[1]
