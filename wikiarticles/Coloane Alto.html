Coloane Alto (Chinese: 疊石塘山; pinyin: Diéshí Tángshān; Cantonese Yale: Daahpsehk Tòhngsāan, Portuguese: Alto de Coloane), at 172.4 metres (560 ft) above sea level, is the highest point of Macau, China. It is located on the island of Coloane.
