Dejan Pešić (Serbian Cyrillic: Дejaн Пeшић ; born December 16, 1976 in Vlasotince) is a Serbian former football goalkeeper.[1]

Honours[edit]

Red Star Belgrade
  • First League of Serbia and Montenegro (2): 1999–00, 2000–01
  • Serbia and Montenegro Cup (2): 1999–00, 2001–02
