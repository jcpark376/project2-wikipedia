Mahila College, Khagaul is a degree college in Bihar, India.[1] It is a constituent unit of Patliputra University.[2] The college offers Senior secondary education and Undergraduate degree in arts, science and conducts some vocational courses.

History[edit]

The college was established in 1972. It became a constituent unit of Patliputra University in 2018.[3]

Degrees and courses[edit]

The college offers the following degrees and courses.[4]

  • Senior Secondary
    • Intermediate of Arts
    • Intermediate of Science
  • Bachelor's degree
    • Bachelor of Arts
    • Bachelor of Science
  • Vocational courses
    • Bachelor of Computer Application
    • Bachelor of Business Management
