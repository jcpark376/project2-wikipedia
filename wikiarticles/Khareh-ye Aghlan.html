Khareh-ye Aghlan (Persian: خره اغلان‎, also Romanized as Khareh-ye Āghlān) is a village in Baryaji Rural District, in the Central District of Sardasht County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 23, in 6 families.[1]
