Anugrah Memorial B.Ed. College, Gaya (A.M. B.Ed. College Gaya; Hindi: अनुग्रह मेमोरियल बीएड महाविद्यालय, गया) is an educational institution offering undergraduate academic degree in Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.), located in Gaya, Bihar, India and is the [1] first B. Ed. unit established in any constituent college of Magadh University.It is recognized by N.C.T.E., government of India. Prof. (Dr.) Lakshmi Narayan Singh, a [2] professor of repute, is the Director of the institute.
