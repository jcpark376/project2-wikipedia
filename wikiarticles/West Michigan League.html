West Michigan League

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The West Michigan League was an early 20th century minor league that included baseball teams in the western part of Michigan. Among the league's teams were the Traverse City Resorters, which folded after the 1915 season, the Cadillac Chiefs, Holland Wooden Shoes, and Muskegon Speed Boys.
