Brayan Enrique Palmezano Reyes (born 17 September 2000) is a Venezuelan footballer who plays as a forward for Chilean Primera División side Huachipato.[1]

Club career[edit]

Palmezano made his senior debut for Zulia in a 2016 Copa Venezuela game against Deportivo Lara in September 2016, 4 days after his 16th birthday. He scored his first goal for the club in a 2–0 cup victory over Estudiantes de Caracas.[2] He scored against another Caracas-based side, Caracas FC, in the league the following season, a first-minute goal in a 3–0 win.[3]

International career[edit]

In April 2017, Palmezano was called up to the Venezuela under-20 side for the first time.[4]
