Bray, Botswana

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Location of Bray

Bray is a village in Kgalagadi District of Botswana. It is located in the eastern part of the district, on the border with South Africa opposite a village of the same name in that country. The population was 1,041 in the 2011 census.[1] It has a primary school.
