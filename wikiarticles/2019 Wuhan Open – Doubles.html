Main article: 2019 Wuhan Open

Elise Mertens and Demi Schuurs were the defending champions,[1] but chose not to participate together. Schuurs teamed up with Anna-Lena Grönefeld, but lost in the semifinals to Mertens and Aryna Sabalenka.

Duan Yingying and Veronika Kudermetova won the title, defeating Mertens and Sabalenka in the final, 7–6(7–3), 6–2.

Seeds[edit]

The top four seeds received a bye into the second round.

  1. Hsieh Su-wei / Barbora Strýcová (Second round)
  2. Elise Mertens / Aryna Sabalenka (Final)
  3. Gabriela Dabrowski / Xu Yifan (Second round)
  4. Anna-Lena Grönefeld / Demi Schuurs (Semifinals)
  5. Samantha Stosur / Zhang Shuai (Second round)
  6. Chan Hao-ching / Latisha Chan (Quarterfinals)
  7. Nicole Melichar / Květa Peschke (Quarterfinals)
  8. Duan Yingying / Veronika Kudermetova (Champions)
