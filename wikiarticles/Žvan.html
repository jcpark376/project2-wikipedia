Žvan (Macedonian: Жван) is a village in the municipality of Demir Hisar, North Macedonia. It used to be part of the former municipality of Sopotnica.

Demographics[edit]

According to the 2002 census, the village had a total of 428 inhabitants.[1] Ethnic groups in the village include:[1]

  • Macedonians 428
