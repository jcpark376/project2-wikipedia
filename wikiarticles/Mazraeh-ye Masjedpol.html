Mazraeh-ye Masjedpol (Persian: مزرعه مسجدپل‎, also Romanized as Mazra‘eh-ye Masjedpol) is a village in Rudbar Rural District, in the Central District of Damghan County, Semnan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[1]
