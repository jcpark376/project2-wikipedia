Lydian Nadhaswaram is a young Indian pianist from Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.[1][2] In 2019, Lydian won the CBS talent show The World's Best and the associated $1 million prize money.[3][4][5] He is the son of Tamil music director Varshan Sathish.[6][7][8].

Lydian spent 4 years studying at KM Music Conservatory, a higher education institution founded in 2008 by the AR Rahman Foundation. [9]

In September 2019, it was announced that Lydian would make his debut as a film composer with the Indian Malayalam language fantasy film Barroz: Guardian of D'Gama's Treasure, directed by Mohanlal, starring himself in the lead.[10]
