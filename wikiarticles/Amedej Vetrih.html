Amedej Vetrih (born 16 September 1990) is a Slovenian footballer who plays as a midfielder for Turkish club Gaziantep.[1]

Club career[edit]

Vetrih was signed by Italian club Parma in a two-year deal[2] in summer 2014 from Gorica. He immediately returned to Nova Gorica in a temporary deal.[3]

On 25 June 2015 Vetrih became a free agent, after the bankruptcy of Parma.

In summer 2015 Vetrih joined Domžale in a two-year deal.[4][5]
