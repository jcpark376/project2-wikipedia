Hergnies is a commune in the Nord department of northern France.

Heraldry[edit]

 The arms of Hergniesare blazoned :

Gules, 10 lozenges conjoined argent 3,3,3 and 1. (Fressain, Hergnies, Lallaing and Marpent use the same arms.)
