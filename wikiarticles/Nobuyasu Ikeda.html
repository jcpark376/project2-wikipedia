Nobuyasu Ikeda (池田 伸康, Ikeda Nobuyasu, born 18 May 1970) is a former Japanese football player.

Playing career[edit]

Ikeda was born in Saitama on 18 May 1970. After graduating from Waseda University, he joined his local club Urawa Reds in 1993. He played many matches as offensive midfielder. However his opportunity to play decreased from 1997. In 2000, he moved to newly was promoted to J1 League club, Kawasaki Frontale. However he could hardly play in the match and he moved to J2 League club Mito HollyHock in 2001. He retired end of 2002 season.

Personal life[edit]

Ikeda married wrestler Miyuu Yamamoto in 1995. They divorced in 1999.
