Ren Fujimura (藤村 怜, Fujimura Ren, born May 26, 1999) is a Japanese football player.[1] He plays for Hokkaido Consadole Sapporo.

Career[edit]

Ren Fujimura promoted J1 League club Hokkaido Consadole Sapporo from youth team in 2017. On May 3, he debuted in J.League Cup (v FC Tokyo).[2][3]
