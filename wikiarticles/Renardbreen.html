Renardbreen is situated between the ridges of Bohlinryggen (left) and Activekammen (right).
LocationWedel Jarlsberg Land at Spitsbergen, Svalbard, Norway
Length8 kilometers

Renardbreen (The Fox Glacier) is a glacier in Wedel Jarlsberg Land at Spitsbergen, Svalbard. The glacier has a length of about 8 kilometers (5.0 mi). It is located between the ridges of Activekammen and Bohlinryggen, and debouches into Recherche Fjord.[1][2]
