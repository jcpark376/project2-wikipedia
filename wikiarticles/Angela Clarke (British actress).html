Angela Clarke (born 1969 in Liverpool, Lancashire) is an English actress[1] best known for playing Colette Daly in the television drama series Soldier Soldier.[2] She has also appeared in Brookside, Coronation Street, Liverpool 1, The Bill, Pie in the Sky, Family Affairsand Casualty.[3]

She is the younger sister of actress Margi Clarke, with whom she appeared in the films Letter to Brezhnev[4] (1985) and Blonde Fist (1991), and screenwriter Frank Clarke (Brookside and Letter to Brezhnev).[5][6]
