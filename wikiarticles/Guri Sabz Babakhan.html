Guri Sabz Babakhan (Persian: گوري سبزباباخان‎, also Romanized as Gūrī Sabz Bābākhān) is a village in Miyankuh-e Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Pol-e Dokhtar County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 13, in 4 families.[1]
