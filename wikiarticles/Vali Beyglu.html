Vali Beyglu (Persian: ولي بيگلو‎, also Romanized as Valī Beyglū; also known as Valī Beyklū)[1] is a village in Angut-e Gharbi Rural District, Anguti District, Germi County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 218, in 43 families.[2]
