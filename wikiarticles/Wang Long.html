Wang Long

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Chinese handball player
This article is about the handball player. For the fictional character, see Wang Lung.

Wang Long (born 27 November 1985) is a Chinese handball player who competed in the 2008 Summer Olympics.[1]
