Klaineanthus is a genus of plants, under the family Euphorbiaceae. There is only one known species, Klaineanthus gaboniae, native to central Africa (Nigeria, Cameroon, Gabon, Zaire, São Tomé & Príncipe).[1][2][3][4][5]
