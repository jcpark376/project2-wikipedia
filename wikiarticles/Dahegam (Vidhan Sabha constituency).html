Dahegam (Vidhan Sabha constituency)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Dahegam assembly constituency (દહેગામ વિધાનસભા બેઠક) is one of the 182 assembly constituency of Gujarat. It is located in Gandhinagar District. The seat came into existence after 2008 delimitation.
