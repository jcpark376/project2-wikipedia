Střelice is a village and municipality (obec) in Znojmo District in the South Moravian Region of the Czech Republic.

The municipality covers an area of 12.15 square kilometres (4.69 sq mi), and has a population of 155 (as at 3 July 2006).

Střelice lies on the Jevišovka River, approximately 18 kilometres (11 mi) north of Znojmo, 52 km (32 mi) south-west of Brno, and 165 km (103 mi) south-east of Prague.
