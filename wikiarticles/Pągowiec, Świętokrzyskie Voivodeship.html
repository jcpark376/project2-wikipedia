Pągowiec [pɔŋˈɡɔvjɛt͡s] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Raków, within Kielce County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 1 kilometre (1 mi) west of Raków and 38 km (24 mi) south-east of the regional capital Kielce.[1]

The village has a population of 78.
