Caelia, or, The Perjur'd Lover is a 1732 comedy play by the British writer Charles Johnson.[1] The play's epilogue was written by Henry Fielding.[2]

The original Drury Lane cast included Jane Cibber as Caelia, John Mills as Meanwell, Thomas Hallam as Bellamy, Roger Bridgewater as Lovemore, William Mills as Wronglove, Edward Berry as Gentleman, John Harper as the Constable and Charlotte Charke as Mrs Lupine.
