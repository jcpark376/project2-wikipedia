Gainesway is a neighborhood in southeastern Lexington, Kentucky, United States. Its boundaries are New Circle Road to the north, Tates Creek Road to the west, Armstrong Mill Road to the south, and a combination of Pimlico Parkway and Bold Bidder Drive to the east.[1]

Neighborhood statistics[edit]

  • Population: 2,014
  • Land area: 0.446 square miles (1.16 km2)
  • Population density: 4,512 people per square mile
  • Median household income: $30,428 (2010)[1]
