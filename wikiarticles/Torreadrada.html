Torreadrada is a municipality located in the province of Segovia, Castile and León, Spain. The town is located about 83 kilometers northeast of Segovia, between Castro de Fuentidueña and Aldeanueva de la Serrezuela.[2] According to the 2010 census (INE), the municipality had a population of 105 inhabitants.[3]
