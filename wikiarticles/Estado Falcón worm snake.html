The Estado Falcón worm snake (Amerotyphlops lehneri ) is a species of snake in the family Typhlopidae. It is endemic to Venezuela.[1][4]

Etymology[edit]

The specific name, lehneri, is in honor of geologist Ernst Lehner.[5]

Geographic range[edit]

A. lehneri is found in the Venezuelan state of Falcón.[1][4]

Reproduction[edit]

A. lehneri is oviparous.[4]
