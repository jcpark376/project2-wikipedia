The ESP James Hetfield (or just ESP JH) are ranges of electric guitars produced by ESP Guitars based on the custom models of James Hetfield. All models were built by James Hetfield and Matt Masciandaro.

Detailed overview[edit]

ESP JH-1

  • Mahogany Body
  • Rosewood Fingerboard
  • Sperzel Locking Tuners
  • Abalone Ninja star point inlays, with full ninja star on first fret
  • Black Hardware
  • Bridge: Tune-o-Matic Bridge w/ Stop Tailpiece
  • 22 XJ Frets
  • EMG 81 (B) / 60 (N) Active p.u.
  • Controls: Three Way Pickup Selector Switch, 2 Volume No Tone Control
  • Finish: Black w/ red hot rod flames

ESP JH-2

  • Mahogany Body

  • Mahogany Neck

  • 42mm Bone Nut
  • Thin U Neck Contour

  • Black Hardware
