Gentian Mezani (born 13 October 1979) is an Albanian football coach who currently is the manager of SC Gjilani in the Football Superleague of Kosovo.[1]

Coaching career[edit]

Flamurtari Vlorë[edit]

Mezani was named the new coach of Flamurtari Vlorë on 25 October 2016 following the departure of Gugash Magani.[1] The club administrator Sinan Idrizi stated that Mezani would remain coach of Flamurtari until the end of 2016–17 season.[2] In his debut match two days later, Flamurtari drew Tërbuni Pukë 3–3 thanks to a last-minute equalizer by Tomislav Bušić in the first leg of 2016–17 Albanian Cup's second round.[3] In the post-match interview, Mezani said that draw was a "fair result".[2]
