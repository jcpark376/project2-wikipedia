Augustinus Kim Jong Soo (Korean: 김종수 아우구스티노; born February 8, 1956) is a Roman Catholic prelate who serves as auxiliary bishop of the Diocese of Daejeon.[2][1]

Biography[edit]

Kim was born in daeheung-dong, Daejeon, South Korea and ordained a priest on February 13, 1989.[2][1] On February 10, 2009, Pope Benedict XVI appointed him Auxiliary Bishop of Daejeon and Titular Bishop of Sufasar.[2][1] On March 3, 2009,[1] he was consecrated by Joseph Kyeong Kap-ryong, retired Bishop of Daejeon with Lazzaro You Heung-sik, Bishop of Daejeon and Cardinal Nicolas Cheong Jin-suk, Archbishop of Seoul as Co-Consecrators.[2]
