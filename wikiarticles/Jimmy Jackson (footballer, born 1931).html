James Jackson (26 March 1931 – 2 March 2013) was a Scottish footballer who played mainly for Notts County.

Jackson made over 120 appearances for Notts County, scoring 50 goals, in two spells with the club. In between, he briefly played in Canada for Toronto.[1]
