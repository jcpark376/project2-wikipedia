Tolombeh-ye Salehi Deh Khan (Persian: تلمبه صالحي ده خان‎, also Romanized as Tolombeh-ye Şāleḥī Deh Khān) is a village in Kavirat Rural District, Chatrud District, Kerman County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 45, in 8 families.[1]
