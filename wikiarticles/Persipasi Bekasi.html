Persatuan Sepak bola Indonesia Patriot Bekasi (Football Association of Indonesia of Patriot Bekasi) is an Indonesian football club based in Bekasi, West Java. Club played in Liga Nusantara. The team plays at Patriot Stadium in downtown Bekasi.
