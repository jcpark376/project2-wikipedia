Lochousice is a village and municipality (obec) in Plzeň-North District in the Plzeň Region of the Czech Republic.

The municipality covers an area of 10.61 square kilometres (4.10 sq mi), and has a population of 111 (as at 2 October 2006).

Lochousice lies approximately 22 kilometres (14 mi) west of Plzeň and 106 km (66 mi) south-west of Prague.
