Reza Aliari (Persian: رضا علیاری; born March 15, 1994‎) is an Iranian football Defender, who currently played for Gol Gohar Sirjan in the Persian Gulf Pro League.

Career[edit]

Early years[edit]

He started his career with Shahrdari Ghods. He also played at Rah Ahan youth levels. Later he joined Moghavemat Tehran U21 and helped them to become champions in 2013–14 U-21 Tehran Asia Vision Premier League.[1]

Naft Tehran[edit]

He joined Naft Tehran on May 26, 2014 with a three-year contract.[2] He made his debut against Naft MIS on September 20, 2014 as a substitute for Kamal Kamyabinia[3] and scored 3 minutes after his entrance.[4]

Saipa[edit]

On may 2017 he joined Saipa and played Under Ali Daei For this season.
