Terry McAuliffe (priest)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Australian Anglican priest

  • Christianity portal

Terry McAuliffe is an Anglican priest,[1] and a former Archdeacon of Forrest then Stirling, WA in the Anglican Diocese of Perth.[2]
