Main article: Shooting at the 2018 Asian Games

The men's 10 metre running target event at the 2018 Asian Games in Jakarta, Indonesia took place on 23–24 August at the Jakabaring International Shooting Range.[1]

Records[edit]

Prior to this competition, the existing world, Asian and Games records were as follows.

World Record  Manfred Kurzer (GER) 590 Athens, Greece 19 August 2004
Asian Record  Zhai Yujia (CHN) 590 Guangzhou, China 16 November 2010
Games Record  Zhai Yujia (CHN) 590 Guangzhou, China 16 November 2010
