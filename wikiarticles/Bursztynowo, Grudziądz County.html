Bursztynowo [burʂtɨˈnɔvɔ] (German: Fürstenau, Kreis Graudenz) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Świecie nad Osą, within Grudziądz County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland.[1] It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) south-west of Świecie nad Osą, 22 km (14 mi) south-east of Grudziądz, and 51 km (32 mi) north-east of Toruń.

The village has a population of 420.
