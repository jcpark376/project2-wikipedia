The 1958–59 Greek Football Cup was the 17th edition of The Greek Football Cup, or Greek Football Cup.

The competition culminated with the Greek Cup Final, held at Apostolos Nikolaidis Stadium, Athens on 5 July 1959. The match was contested by Olympiacos and Doxa Drama, with Olympiacos winning 2–1.[1]
