Noel Van Raalte

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Noel Marcus van Raalte (22 December 1888, in Paddington, London – 5 May 1940, in Kensington, London) was a British racecar driver. A wealthy playboy whose peerage family owned Brownsea Island, he raced in the 1915 Indianapolis 500 in a Sunbeam, finishing in 10th place.[1] He later owned the first Bentley automobile.[2]
