1982 Kensington and Chelsea London Borough Council election

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The 1982 Kensington and Chelsea Council election took place on 6 May 1982 to elect members of Kensington and Chelsea London Borough Council in London, England. The whole council was up for election and the Conservative party stayed in overall control of the council.[1]
