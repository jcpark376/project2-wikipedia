Deval Khedi is a village in the Bhopal district of Madhya Pradesh, India. It is located in the Huzur tehsil and the Phanda block.[1]

Demographics[edit]

According to the 2011 census of India, Deval Khedi has 136 households. The effective literacy rate (i.e. the literacy rate of population excluding children aged 6 and below) is 68.85%.[2]
