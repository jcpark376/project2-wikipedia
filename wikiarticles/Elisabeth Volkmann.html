Elisabeth Volkmann

Elisabeth Volkmann (German: [e.ˈliː.za.bɛt ˈfɔlkˌman] (listen); 16 March 1936 – 27 July 2006)[1] was a German actress and voice actor, best known for her part in the German absurd comedy series Klimbim [de] (1973–1979), which was watched by millions of viewers in Germany[2] and, later on, as the voice of Marge Simpson in the German dub of The Simpsons. Volkmann was born in Essen and died in Munich.
