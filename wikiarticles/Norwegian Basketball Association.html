The Norwegian Basketball Association (Norwegian: Norges Basketballforbund) also known as NBBF is the governing body for basketball in Norway. It was formed in 1966, and joined FIBA in 1968. They are headquartered in Oslo.

The Norwegian Basketball Association operates the Norway men's national team and Norway women's national team. They organize national competitions in Norway, for both the men's and women's senior teams and also the youth national basketball teams.[1]

The top level professional league in Norway is the BLNO.

As of 2020, its president has been Hendrik Parmann.
