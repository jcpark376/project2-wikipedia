Joice Cristina de Souza Rodrigues (born September 6, 1986)[1] is a Brazilian female basketball player.[2] At the 2012 Summer Olympics, she competed for the Brazil women's national basketball team in the women's event. She is 5 ft 9 in (1.75 m) tall.
