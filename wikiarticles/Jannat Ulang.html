Jannat Ulang (Persian: جنت اولنگ‎, also Romanized as Jannat Ūlang; also known as Jannat Olang)[1] is a village in Qanibeyglu Rural District, Zanjanrud District, Zanjan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[2]
