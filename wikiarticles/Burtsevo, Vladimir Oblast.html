Burtsevo (Russian: Бурцево) is a rural locality (a village) in Styopantsevskoye Rural Settlement, Vyaznikovsky District, Vladimir Oblast, Russia. The population was 11 as of 2010.[2]

Geography[edit]

The village is located 2 km south from Styopantsevo, 42 km south-west from Vyazniki.
