John Reece (born March 1957) is a British billionaire businessman.

Early life[edit]

Reece was born in March 1957.[2] He was educated at Bede's School, Sunderland (now Sunderland College), and studied economics at Queens' College, Cambridge.[1][3]

Career[edit]

In 2000, Reece, an accountant and partner at PricewaterhouseCoopers, joined the chemicals conglomerate Ineos, and is now the chief financial officer and minority shareholder.[1]

Personal life[edit]

Reece lives in Bougy-Villars, Switzerland.[1]
