Kochnegovskaya (Russian: Кочнеговская) is a rural locality (a village) in Nikolskoye Rural Settlement of Vilegodsky District, Arkhangelsk Oblast, Russia. The population was 11 as of 2010.[2]

Geography[edit]

It is located 1.2 km from Nikolsk.
