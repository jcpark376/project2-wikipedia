"Makhna" is a hit single by the Indian rapper Yo Yo Honey Singh and Neha Kakkar.[1][2] The track was released on 21 December 2018 as a single by T-Series via YouTube.[3] The video features Nidhi Sunil and Yo Yo Honey Singh. It's one the most expensive single music video in India and is shot in Old Havana, Cuba.

Music video[edit]

The musical video marks the comeback song of the rapper after the extended break of 4 years due to his Bipolar disorder. The musical video is produced by Bhushan Kumar of T-Series and co-produced by Bobby Suri and Yo Yo Honey Singh and is directed by Daniel Duran.

Reception[edit]

The song has received commercial success, peaking at the number one position on YouTube India views, crossing 20 million views in 24 hours of its release,[4][5] It is currently the most commented Indian music video song on YouTube. As of 16 August 2020, it has over 271 million views on YouTube with over 3.4 milion likes.
