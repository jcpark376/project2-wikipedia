Dorungar Rural District (Persian: دهستان درونگر‎) is a rural district (dehestan) in Now Khandan District, Dargaz County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 3,296, in 896 families.[1] The rural district has 16 villages.
