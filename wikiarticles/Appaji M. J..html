Appaji M. J. is an Indian Politician from the state of Karnataka. He is a three term member of the Karnataka Legislative Assembly.[1]

Constituency[edit]

He represents the Bhadravati, Karnataka constituency.[2]

Political Party[edit]

He is from the Janata Dal (Secular).[3][4][5]
