Devayani Farande

Devayani Suhas Pharande is an Indian female politician and member of the Bharatiya Janata Party. Pharande is a member of the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly from the Nashik Central constituency in Nashik district and was elected to the seat in 2014 Maharashtra Legislative Assembly election.[1][2][3]
