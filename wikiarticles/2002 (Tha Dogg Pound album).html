2002 is the first compilation album by the rap group Tha Dogg Pound.[4] It comprises unreleased material recorded by Tha Dogg Pound while they were signed on to Death Row Records,[5] remixed and updated by the then-head producer Cold 187um.[6] Tha Dogg Pound were signed to Dillinger's D.P.G. Recordz and released their own album that year called Dillinger & Young Gotti. The album title is meant to imply it is a sequel or response of sorts to Dr. Dre's 2001.[7] The album cover has pictures of Daz and Kurupt from the Death Row family picture.
