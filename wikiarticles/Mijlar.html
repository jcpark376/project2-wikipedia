Mijlar (Persian: ميجلار‎, also Romanized as Mījlār; also known as Mejel and Mejlār)[1] is a village in Kuhestan Rural District, Kelardasht District, Chalus County, Mazandaran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 85, in 27 families.[2]
