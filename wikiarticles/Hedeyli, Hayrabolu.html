Hedeyli is a village in Hayrabolu district of Tekirdağ Province, Turkey, It is situated in the eastern Trakya (Thrace) plains at 40°19′N 27°1′E﻿ / ﻿40.317°N 27.017°E﻿ / 40.317; 27.017. The distance to Hayrabolu is 18 kilometres (11 mi) . The population of the village is 331 as of 2011.[1] It was a Bulgarian village during the Ottoman Empire era. But after the Second Balkan War the Bulgarian population was forced to leave the settlement.
