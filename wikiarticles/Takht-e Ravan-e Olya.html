Takht-e Ravan-e Olya (Persian: تختروان عليا‎, also Romanized as Takht-e Ravān-e ‘Olyā)[1] is a village in Chaldoran-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 410, in 68 families.[2]
