Amir Baitukanov (born 29 October 1989)[1] is a Kazakhstani long-distance runner.

In 2018, he competed in the men's half marathon at the 2018 IAAF World Half Marathon Championships held in Valencia, Spain.[2] He finished in 133rd place.[2]
