Shawo Township (simplified Chinese: 沙窝乡; traditional Chinese: 沙窩鄉; pinyin: Shāwō Xiāng) is a township of Echeng District, Ezhou, Hubei, People's Republic of China,[2] located 5 kilometres (3.1 mi) south of the Yangtze River and almost twice that southeast of downtown Ezhou.
