William Michael Hegarty (born June 30, 1931 in Medford, Massachusetts) is a former American football offensive tackle in the National Football League for the Pittsburgh Steelers and the Washington Redskins. He played college football at the University of Georgia and Villanova University[1] and was drafted in the fifteenth round of the 1952 NFL Draft by the Los Angeles Rams.
