Virginia Sandhya Peiris (born 27 November 1952 as වීණා ජයකොඩි) [Sinhala]), better known as Veena Jayakody, is a actress in Sri Lankan cinema, stage drama and television.[1][2] Started as a child artist, Jayakody has played critically acclaimed roles in films Sagarayak Meda, Ra Manamali and Sarungalaya.

Personal life[edit]

Veena Jayakodi was born on 27 November 1952. Her mother Rohini Jayakody was a popular actress and a film director.[3] She has three children - one daughter and two sons. Eldest daughter is Anjana, eldest son is Chandu and youngest son is Sanjay. Anjana is married to Srini and they have two children - Waishya and Kaushik. Chandu is married to Melonie and the couple has three children - Jason, Tharaa and Joshua.

Acting career[edit]

Jayakody started her film career with Ahankara Sthree back in 1954, directed by A. B. Raj as a child with her mother.[3] At the age of 12, Jayakody acted in 1968 film Hangi Hora directed by her mother Rohini Jayakody, where she acted as a dancer. At the age of 14, she acted in a Vesak drama Kohomada Wade.[3]
