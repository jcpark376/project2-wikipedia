Roco Kingdom 3 (Chinese: 洛克王国3：圣龙的守护) is a 2014 Chinese animated fantasy adventure film directed by Gong Bingsi and part of the Roco Kingdom film series. It was released on 10 July 2014.[2] It was preceded by Roco Kingdom: The Desire of Dragon (2013) and it was followed by Roco Kingdom 4 (2015).
