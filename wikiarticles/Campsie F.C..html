Campsie Football Club was a Scottish association football club based in the village of Milton of Campsie, Stirlingshire. The club was founded in 1883 and disbanded in 1894. The club competed in the Scottish Cup for nine seasons between 1883 and 1892 as well as the regional Stirlingshire Cup competition.[1][2] The club's home colours were navy blue until 1891 when they changed to light blue.[1]
