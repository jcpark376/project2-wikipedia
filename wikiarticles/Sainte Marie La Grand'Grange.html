Sainte Marie La Grand’Grange in Saint-Chamond, Loire, France, is a primary through secondary school founded by the Marist Fathers in 1850, and currently run by the Society of Jesus.

Rankings[edit]

The middle school enjoys a near-perfect passing rate (99.5-100%).[1] The secondary school was rated by l'express as 2nd among 29 schools in the Loire department in 2016.[2]
