Pyar Hua Chori Chori is a 1991 Hindi-language Indian feature film directed by K.Bapaiah, starring Mithun Chakraborty, Gouthami, Shikha Swaroop, Shafi Inamdar, Shakti Kapoor and Anupam Kher[1] This was Gouthami's debut Hindi film. This is the remake of Malayalam Blockbuster Chithram starring Mohanlal and directed by Priyadarshan.

Plot[edit]

Pyar Hua Chori Chori is the story of a simple man Vijay (Mithun), looking for quick money for his son's operation. He was offered the "Job" to act as a husband for a rich, wealthy girl Radha (Gouthami) due to circumstances. The make-believe marriage finally ends up in love with each other. But Vijay has a dark past, which would hurt every one. Can he manage to survive?
