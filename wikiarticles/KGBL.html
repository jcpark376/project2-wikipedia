KGBL (100.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Lakin, Kansas. The station is owned by Steckline Communications, Inc., and airs a country music format.[2]

The station was assigned the call sign KGRQ by the Federal Communications Commission on September 23, 2009. The station changed its call sign to KGBL on March 1, 2014.[1]
