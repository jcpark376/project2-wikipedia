Qeshlaq-e Alapapakh (Persian: قشلاق الاپاپاخ‎, also Romanized as Qeshlāq-e Ālāpāpākh) is a village in Qeshlaq-e Gharbi Rural District, Aslan Duz District, Parsabad County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 111, in 21 families.[1]
