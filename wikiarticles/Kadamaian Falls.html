Kadamaian Falls

Kadamaian Waterfall is a cascading waterfall approximately 700 m (2,300 ft) high on the southern flank of the Mount Kinabalu. It is the longest waterfall in Malaysia and can be seen from the Tamparuli-Ranau road. However it is not easily accessible.
