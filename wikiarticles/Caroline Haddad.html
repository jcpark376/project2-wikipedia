Caroline Haddad is a Canadian former competitive pair skater who currently works as a coach. With Jean-Sébastien Fecteau, she is the 1992 and 1994 World Junior silver medallist and the 1993 Nebelhorn Trophy champion.[1]

Haddad is now a coach. She has coached Steza Foo / David Struthers[2] and Cathy Harvey / Jean-Marc Babin.[3]
