Western Suburbs SC
Full nameWestern Suburbs Soccer Club

The Western Suburbs Soccer Club is a defunct Australian association football club that was based in Sydney. The club was a foundation member of Australia's National Soccer League in 1977,[1] and had previously played in the NSW Premier League (then known as NSW Division 1) and the NSW State League Division One (then known as NSW Division 2). Western Suburbs amalgamated with APIA Leichhardt Tigers FC at the end of 1978 NSL season.[2]

Honours[edit]

  • Ampol Cup Winner: 1971, 1976.[3]
  • NSW State League Runner-Up: 1971 (lost grand final to St George Budapest 3–2).[4]
  • Federation Cup Runner-Up: 1975.[3]
  • NSW Division 1 Champion: 1970. [5]

Notable former players[edit]

  • Peter Wilson[2]
  • Colin Curran
  • Dave Harding
