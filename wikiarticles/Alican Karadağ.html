Alican Karadağ (born 8 January 1990) is a Turkish professional footballer who most recently played as a midfielder for Kastamonuspor 1966.

Professional career[edit]

Karadağ spent most of his playing career in the TFF First League and TFF Second League, playing over 120 games in the lower divisions of Turkey.[1] In 8 January 2018, he signed with Kardemir Karabükspor after a successful half season at Kastamonuspor where he scored 5 goals in 12 games.[2]Karadağ made his professional debut with Kardemir Karabükspor in a 3-2 Süper Lig loss to Kayserispor on 11 March 2018.[3]
