Old Style leap year starting on Saturday

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

This is the calendar for any Old Style leap year starting on Saturday, 25 March. The Old Style calendar ended the following March, on 24 March. Examples: Julian year 1564, 1620 or 1704 (see bottom tables).

A leap year is a year with 366 days, as compared to a common year, which has 365.
