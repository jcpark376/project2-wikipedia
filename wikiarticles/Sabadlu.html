Sabadlu (Persian: سبدلو‎, also Romanized as Sabadlū; also known as Sabadly)[1] is a village in Shuy Rural District, in the Central District of Baneh County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 896, in 209 families.[2]
