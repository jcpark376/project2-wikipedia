Shahram Nikbakht Bidgoli (Sean Nikbakht) is an Iranian professional bodybuilder. In 2016, he became the champion in bodybuilding competitions Physics America Championship.[1] Then he won several competitions in professional bodybuilding world-class.

Biography[edit]

Shahram Nikbakht شهرام نیکبخت بیدگلی was born on June 23, 1970, in Tehran. He started his sports activity in 1986 with wrestling. From 1996, he continued his activity as a bodybuilding coach and He succeeded in being officially appointed as the coach of Iran's national wrestling teams in 1999.

Education[edit]

Shahram Nikbakht has a master's degree in physical education and has completed several coaching and fitness courses in Italy and Germany.

Bodybuilding[edit]

His success as a professional bodybuilder has led to many product endorsements and other opportunities in his career. Shahram Nikbakht has participated in more than 25 Olympic, world and Asian competitions, including the Athens Olympics, the World Championships in the United States, Hungary, Iran and Azerbaijan. His success as a professional bodybuilder has led to many product endorsements and other opportunities in his career. In 2016, he became the champion of the American Physics Championship in Pittsburgh and entered the Professional field. In 2017, he became the sixteenth person in the California Grand Prix professional competitions,[2] and after participating in the Hawaii Pro professional competitions, he took the thirteenth place. He also became the Eleventh person in the Crystal cup professional competition. In 2018, Shahram Nikbakht won the sixth place in the New Zealand Pro Championship and the third place in the Fiji Pro Championship[3] in his sports history. In 2019, he won the seventh place in the professional competitions of Muscle Contest,[4] Vietnam.[5]
