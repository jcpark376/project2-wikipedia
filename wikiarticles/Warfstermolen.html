Warfstermolen (West Frisian: Warfstermûne) (Low Saxon: Warfstermeul) is a small village in Noardeast-Fryslân municipality, in the province of Friesland, the Netherlands.[1] It had a population of 203 in January 2017.[2] Before 2019, the village was part of the Kollumerland en Nieuwkruisland municipality.
