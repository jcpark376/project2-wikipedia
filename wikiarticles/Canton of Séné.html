Canton of Séné

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The canton of Séné is an administrative division of the Morbihan department, northwestern France. It was created at the French canton reorganisation which came into effect in March 2015. Its seat is in Séné.[1]

It consists of the following communes:[1]

  1. Arzon
  2. Le Hézo
  3. Saint-Armel
  4. Saint-Gildas-de-Rhuys
  5. Sarzeau
  6. Séné
  7. Surzur
  8. Theix-Noyalo
  9. Le Tour-du-Parc
  10. La Trinité-Surzur
