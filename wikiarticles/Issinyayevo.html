Issinyayevo (Russian: Иссиняево) is a rural locality (a village) in Zaboryinskoye Rural Settlement, Beryozovsky District, Perm Krai, Russia. The population was 65 as of 2010.[2]

Geography[edit]

It is located on the Shakva River.
