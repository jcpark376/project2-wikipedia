Parsabad (Persian: پارس اباد‎, also Romanized as Pārsābād; also known as Parsavat and Partovū)[1] is a village in Sanjabad-e Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Kowsar County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[2]
