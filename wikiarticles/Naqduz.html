Naqduz (Persian: نقدوز‎, also Romanized as Naqdūz; also known as Nīāz)[1] is a village in Qeshlaq Rural District, in the Central District of Ahar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 418, in 90 families.[2]
