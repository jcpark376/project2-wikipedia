Nedyu Rachev
Personal information
Born(1915-10-04)4 October 1915

Nedyu Rachev (Bulgarian: Недю Рачев, born 4 October 1915, date of death unknown) was a Bulgarian cyclist. He competed in the sprint event at the 1936 Summer Olympics.[1]
