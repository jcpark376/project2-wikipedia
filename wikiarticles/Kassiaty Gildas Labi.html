Kassiaty Gildas Aymard Labi (born May 6, 1984 in Abidjan) is an Ivorian football player.

Career[edit]

Labi began his career in 2001 with Africa Sports as midfielder until 2006 and wore the club number 12.[1] The former Africa Captain signed in 2007 for ASEC Mimosas as midfielder[2] wearing club number 8. In January 2009 he left ASEC Mimosas before signing, in May 2009, for Yangon United FC.[3] He played for striker wearing club number 19. In January 2010 he signed for Delta United as a midfielder wearing club number 8 he played in the Myanmar League for about a year and half. After getting a new contract he moved to Thailand and was signed to the first team for league Division 1 in 2011 with Chanthaburi F.C., as striker wearing club number 25. Then in 2012 he signed for a new team with Rayong United F.C., as striker in league Division 2. In January 2013 he moved to league Division 1 again and signed with Trat F.C. as midfielder wearing club number 23.
