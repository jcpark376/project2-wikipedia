Arvibacter is a Gram-negative, aerobic, rod-shaped and motile genus of bacteria from the family of Chitinophagaceae with one known species (Arvibacter flaviflagrans).[1][2][3][4] Arvibacter flaviflagrans has been isolated from forest soil from the Kyonggi University in Korea.[4]
