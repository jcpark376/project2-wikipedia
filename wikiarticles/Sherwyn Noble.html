Sherwyn Noble (born 15 August 1976) is a Guyanese-born Sint Maartener cricketer.

A right-handed batsman and right-arm medium-fast bowler, Noble was selected in Sint Maarten's squad for the 2008 Stanford 20/20. Having received a bye into the first round after Cuba could not fulfill their preliminary round fixture,[1] Dornick made his Twenty20 debut in the first-round match against Saint Vincent and the Grenadines,[2] which despite an unbeaten century from John Eugene, Sint Maarten lost by 10 runs and were eliminated from the tournament.[3] Noble played in this match as a middle-order batsman, though without success as he was dismissed without scoring by Orlanzo Jackson.[4] This marks Noble's only appearance in Twenty20 cricket.
