Polygamy in Kuwait

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Polygamy is legal in Kuwait. In Kuwait, nearly 2% of all marriages are polygamous.[1] It has been reported that polygamy is on the decline among the younger generation.[2]
