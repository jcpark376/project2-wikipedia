Gordon MacKenzie Johnston (born 30 January 1993) is a Canadian field hockey player, who plays as a defender for the Vancouver Hawks and the Canadian national team.

International career[edit]

He competed at the 2015 Pan American Games and won a silver medal.[1][2] In 2016, he was named to Canada's Olympic team.[3] He was selected for the 2018 World Cup,[4] where he played all four games. In June 2019, he was selected in the Canada squad for the 2019 Pan American Games.[5] They won the silver medal as they lost 5–2 to Argentina in the final.[6]
