KYYK (98.3 FM, "Kick 98.3") is a radio station broadcasting a country music format.[1] Licensed to Palestine, Texas, United States, the station serves the Tyler-Jacksonville area. The station is currently owned by TomlinsonâLeis Communications, L.P.[2]

History[edit]

The station was assigned the call sign KYYK on 1981-11-18. On 1987-07-01, the station changed its call sign to KNET-FM and on 1987-09-21, back to the current KYYK.[3]
