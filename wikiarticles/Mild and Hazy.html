Mild and Hazy is a 7" vinyl single by Canadian singer-songwriter Hayden. It was released in 1996 on Hayden's own label, Hardwood Records as well as on Lunamoth.[1] The cover is a photograph of Hayden as a toddler. The song "Gouge Away" is a cover of the Pixies, from their album Doolittle.

Track listing[edit]

All songs written by Paul Hayden Desser, except where noted.

  2. "Mild" – 1:04

  2. "Hazy" – 1:06
