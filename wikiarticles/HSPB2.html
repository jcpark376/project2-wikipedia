Heat shock protein beta-2 is a protein that in humans is encoded by the HSPB2 gene.[4][5][6]

Interactions[edit]

HSPB2 has been shown to interact with:

  • CRYAB,[7]
  • HSPB8,[8][9]
  • Myotonic dystrophy protein kinase[5][7] and
  • TRAF6.[10]
