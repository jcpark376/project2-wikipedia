Polyfyto (Greek: Πολύφυτο) is a village and a community of the Servia-Velventos municipality.[2] Before the 2011 local government reform it was part of the municipality of Velventos, of which it was a municipal district.[2] The 2011 census recorded 7 inhabitants in the village.[1]
