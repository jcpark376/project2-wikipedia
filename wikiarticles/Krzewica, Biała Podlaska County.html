Krzewica [kʂɛˈvʲit͡sa] (Ukrainian: Кревиця, romanized: Krevytsia) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Międzyrzec Podlaski, within Biała Podlaska County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland.[1] It lies approximately 12 kilometres (7 mi) north-west of Międzyrzec Podlaski, 31 km (19 mi) west of Biała Podlaska, and 91 km (57 mi) north of the regional capital Lublin.
