Qeshlaq-e Musa Beyg (Persian: قشلاق موسي بيگ‎, also Romanized as Qeshlāq-e Mūsá Beyg; also known as Qeshlāq-e Mūsá Beyglū)[1] is a village in Tirchai Rural District, Kandovan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 288, in 63 families.[2]
