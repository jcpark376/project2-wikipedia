Tol (or Tolköy) is a small village in Mersin Province, Turkey. It is a part of Mezitli district which is an intracity district of Greater Mersin. It is situated between a creek and a high hill in the Toros Mountains. At 36°49′N 34°23′E﻿ / ﻿36.817°N 34.383°E﻿ / 36.817; 34.383 the distance to Mersin is 28 kilometres (17 mi). It is one of the least populated villages of the province with a population of only 120[1] as of 2012.
