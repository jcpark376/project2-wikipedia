Ivan Aleksandrovich Shtyl (Russian: Иван Александрович Штыль; born 6 August 1986) is a Russian sprint canoeist who has competed since 2004. He won eighteen medals at the ICF Canoe Sprint World Championships with twelve golds (C-1 200 m: 2010, C-1 4 × 200 m: 2009, 2010, 2011, 2013, 2014; C-2 200 m: 2006, 2007, 2014, 2015; C-2 500 m: 2013, 2014) and six silvers (C-1 200 m: 2011, 2013; C-2 200 m: 2009, 2010; C-2 500 m: 2009, C-4 200 m: 2007). He has also won an Olympic bronze medal, at the 2012 Summer Olympics in the men's C-1 200 m.[1]
