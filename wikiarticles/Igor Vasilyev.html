Igor Vladimirovich Vasilyev (Russian: Игорь Владимирович Васильев; born April 1, 1964) is a Russian former handball player who competed for the Unified Team in the 1992 Summer Olympics.

He was born in Volgograd.

In 1992 he won the gold medal with the Unified Team. He played all seven matches and scored two goals.
