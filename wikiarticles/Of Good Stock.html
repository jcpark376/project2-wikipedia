Of Good Stock
Written byMelissa Ross
Directed byLynne Meadow
Date premieredJune 2015 (2015-06)
Place premieredManhattan Theatre Club

Of Good Stock is a play written by Melissa Ross and directed by Lynne Meadow.[1][2] It premiered at Manhattan Theatre Club's off Broadway Mainstage in June 2015.[3]

Synopsis[edit]

The play centres around three sisters, and the men in their lives, who gather at their family home for a summer weekend. Their reunion ignites passion, humour and unanticipated upheavals.[4]
