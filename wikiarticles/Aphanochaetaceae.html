Aphanochaetaceae is a family of algae in the order Chaetophorales.[1]

Genera[2][edit]

  • Aphanochaete Braun
  • Chaetonema Nowakowski [Nowakovsky]
  • Gonatoblaste J.Huber, 1892
  • Micropoa Moewus
  • Thamniochaete F.Gay
