Zajączkowo [zajɔnt͡ʂˈkɔvɔ] is a settlement in the administrative district of Gmina Dziemiany, within Kościerzyna County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland.[1] It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) west of Dziemiany, 23 km (14 mi) south-west of Kościerzyna, and 74 km (46 mi) south-west of the regional capital Gdańsk.

For details of the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.

The settlement has a population of 8.
