Prabhu Bamala Chauhan (b 1969) is an Indian politician who is the current Minister of State for Animal Husbandry, Hajj & waqf board of Karnataka. He is the current MLA from Aurad, and minister in charge of the Bidar - Yadgiri district. [1][3]

He has been elected to Karnataka assembly from Aurad, a reserved constituency, in 2008, 2013, and 2018. Gopinath Munde, former Deputy CM of Maharashtra, was his mentor. Chauhan is the protege of ex-MLA from Aurad, Gundappa Vakil. [4]
