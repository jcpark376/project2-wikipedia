Jordan Hitch is an American business executive, and currently serves as the managing director at Bain Capital Private Equity and Bain Capital, LLC.[1][2]

He holds a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Lehigh University; and an M.B.A., with distinction, from the University of Chicago Graduate School of Business.[1]

He also serves on the board of Giraffe Holding Inc., Guitar Center Holdings Inc., Gymboree Corporation, and Burlington Coat Factory Investments Holdings Inc.

Early life and education[edit]

Hitch was born in the United States. He pursued engineering studies from Lehigh University. After earning a B.S. degree in Mechanical Engineering; he enrolled for an MBA program from University of Chicago Booth School of Business, which he completed with distinction.[1]

Career[edit]

Hitch joined Nalco Chemical Company (presently Nalco Holding Company) as an area manager.[3]

After Nalco, he made his entry into finance as a consultant with Bain & Company; focusing in the financial services, healthcare, and utility industries.[1]

He later joined Bain Capital in 1997, and rose to the post of a managing director at Bain Capital Private Equity and Bain Capital, LLC.

Board[edit]

He serves as a director of Giraffe Holding Inc., and Guitar Center Holdings Inc.[4] He has been a director of the Gymboree Corporation since November 2010,[5] and Burlington Coat Factory Investments Holdings Inc. since April 13, 2006.[6]
