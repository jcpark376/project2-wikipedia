Amagami Hime

"Amagami Hime" (甘噛み姫, Play Biting Princess) is the 14th single by Japanese idol girl group NMB48. It was released on April 27, 2016.[1] It was number-one on the Oricon Weekly Singles Chart[2][3] and ranked 9th with 281,513 copies sold in the first half year of 2016.[4] It was also number-one on the Billboard Japan Hot 100.[5]

The center position in the choreography for the title song is held by Sayaka Yamamoto.[6]
