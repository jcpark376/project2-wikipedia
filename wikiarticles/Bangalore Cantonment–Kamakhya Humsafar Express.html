Bangalore Cantonment–Agartala Humsafar Express was a completely 3-tier AC sleeper trains of the Indian Railways connecting Bangalore Cantonment in Karnataka and Kamakhya Junction in Assam. It was currently being operated with 12503/12504 train numbers on weekly basis. This train was extended to Agartala in Tripura and now run as 12503/12504 Bangalore Cantonment - Agartala Humsafar Express.

Coach Composition[edit]

The trains is completely 3-tier AC LHB Coaches designed by Indian Railways with features of LED screen display to show information about stations, train speed etc. and will have announcement system as well, Vending machines for tea, coffee and milk, Bio toilets in compartments as well as CCTV cameras.

This train consists of sixteen AC III Tier Coaches, one Pantry Car and two Generator Power Car Coaches.

  • 16 AC III Tier
  • 1 Pantry Car
  • 2 Generator Power Car

Loco link[edit]

This train was hauled by WAP 4 of Vijayawada from Bangalore to Visakhapatnam. Then from Visakhapatnam to Howrah it was hauled by WAP 4 of Santragachi. And then from Howrah to Kamakhya it is hauled by Siliguri-based WDP4D/ WDP4B/ WDP4.

Service[edit]

It averages 61 km/hr as 12504 Humsafar Express starts on Tuesday and covering 3004 km in 49 hrs 5 mins & 59 km/hr as 12503 Humsafar Express starts on Friday covering 3005 km in 51 hrs 15 mins.[1]
