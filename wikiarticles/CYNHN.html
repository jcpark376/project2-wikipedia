CYNHN (СУИНИ, スウィーニー) is a Japanese idol girl group formed in 2017. They released their debut single, "Finalegend", on November 1, 2017.[1]

History[edit]

CYNHN debuted in November 2017 with the single, "Finalegend".[1] Their second single, "Haribote", was released in April 2018,[2] followed by their third single, "Takisaikia / So Young", in September 2018, and their fourth single, "Zekkō Kyōshū / Ame Iro Horoguramu", in December 2018.[3] Their fifth single, "Kūki to Ink / Wire", was released in March 2019.[4] They released their first studio album, Tablature, in June 2019.[5] Their sixth single, "2-Ji no Parade", was released in November 2019,[6] followed by their seventh single, "Suisei", in March 2020.[7] They will released their eighth single, "Goku Heibonna Ao wa,", in September 2020.[8]
