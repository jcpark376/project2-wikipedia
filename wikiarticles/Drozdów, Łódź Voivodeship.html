Drozdów [ˈdrɔzduf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Świnice Warckie, within Łęczyca County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland.[1] It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) east of Świnice Warckie, 14 km (9 mi) west of Łęczyca, and 42 km (26 mi) north-west of the regional capital Łódź.
