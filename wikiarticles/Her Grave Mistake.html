Her Grave Mistake is a 1914 silent Western film featuring Lon Chaney. The film is now considered to be lost.[1]

Cast[edit]

  • Murdock MacQuarrie as Roger Grant
  • Agnes Vernon as Isabel Norris
  • Seymour Hastings as Isabel's Father
  • Lon Chaney as Nunez
