Gibeauxiella is a genus of moths in the family Cosmopterigidae.

Species[edit]

  • Gibeauxiella bellaqueifontis (Gibeaux, 1986)
  • Gibeauxiella genitrix (Meyrick, 1927)
  • Gibeauxiella reliqua (Gibeaux, 1986)
