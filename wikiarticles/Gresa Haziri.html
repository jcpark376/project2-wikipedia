Gresa Haziri (born 10 April 1999) is an Albanian footballer who plays as a defender and has appeared for the Albania women's national team.

Career[edit]

Haziri has been capped for the Albania national team, appearing for the team during the 2019 FIFA Women's World Cup qualifying cycle.[1]
