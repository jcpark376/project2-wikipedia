Cathedral of St Mary and St James, Maseru

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

St Mary and St James Cathedral is an Anglican church in Maseru.[1] The current incumbent is The Very Rev Job Mofana.[2]
