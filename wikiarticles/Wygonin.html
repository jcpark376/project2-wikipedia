Wygonin [vɨˈɡɔnin] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Stara Kiszewa, within Kościerzyna County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland.[1] It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south-west of Stara Kiszewa, 20 km (12 mi) south-east of Kościerzyna, and 56 km (35 mi) south-west of the regional capital Gdańsk.

For details of the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.

The village has a population of 40.
