Daisy Liu Waite[1] (born 9 September 2005) is an English-Chinese actress, model and presenter. She is best known for portraying Mei-Xue, in the Chinese 2014 adventure film Balala the Fairies: The Magic Trial.[2] On TV she is known for presenting a regular feature on the Hunan Broadcasting System show Playing Tricks (玩名堂), broadcast nationally on the Aniworld Satellite TV channel.[3][4] Daisy has also modeled for large brands such as ANTA Sports.[3] Daisy was born in Reading, Berkshire, England but has resided in Changsha, Hunan since she was 3 years old.[3]
