The 1955 Iowa State Teachers Panthers football team was an American football team that represented Iowa State Teachers College (later renamed Northern Iowa University) in the North Central Conference during the 1955 college football season. In their 18th season under head coach Clyde Starbeck, the team compiled an 8–1 record (5–1 against NCC opponents) and finished in second place in the NCC.[1]
