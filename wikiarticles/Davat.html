Davat (Persian: دعوت‎, also Romanized as Da‘vat and Dāwāt)[1] is a village in Afin Rural District, Zohan District, Zirkuh County, South Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 80, in 22 families.[2]
