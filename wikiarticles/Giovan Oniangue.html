Giovan Oniangue (born April 22, 1991) is a Congolese professional basketball player for Orléans Loiret Basket of the French Pro A. He signed with Orleans in January 2019, and averaged 7.8 points and 3.1 rebounds per game during the 2019-20 season. On May 29, 2020, he signed a two-year extension.[1]

He represented the Republic of the Congo's national basketball team at the AfroBasket 2013 in Abidjan, Ivory Coast, where he was his team’s top scorer.[2]
