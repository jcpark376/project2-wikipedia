Andy Ling

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search
For the Hong Kong violist, see Andrew Ling.

Andy Ling is a British trance artist and remixer. He became popular due to his "Fixation" single. This reached No. 55 in the UK Singles Chart in 2000, and was released on the Hooj Choons label.[1]
