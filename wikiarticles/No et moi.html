No et moi (lit. 'No and me') is a 2010 French drama film directed and co-written by Zabou Breitman. It is based on the Prix des Libraires-winning novel of the same name by Delphine de Vigan.[3][4]

Cast[edit]

  • Julie-Marie Parmentier as Nora ("No")
  • Nina Rodriguez as Lou Bertignac
  • Antonin Chalon as Lucas
  • Bernard Campan as Lou's father
  • Zabou Breitman as Lou's mother
  • Grégoire Bonnet as Monsieur Vargas
  • Guilaine Londez as Sylvie
  • Eric Valero as Éric
