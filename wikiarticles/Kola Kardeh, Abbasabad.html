Kola Kardeh (Persian: کلاکرده‎, also Romanized as Kolā Kardeh) is a village in Langarud Rural District, Salman Shahr District, Abbasabad County, Mazandaran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 357, in 103 families.[1]
