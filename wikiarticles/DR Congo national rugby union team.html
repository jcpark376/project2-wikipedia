Not to be confused with Republic of the Congo national rugby union team.

The Democratic Republic of the Congo national rugby union team {French: Équipe de République Démocratique du Congo de rugby à XV) is a member of the International Rugby Board (IRB), and have yet to play in a Rugby World Cup tournament.

They were due to appear in the 2008 Castel Beer Trophy, but they were replaced by Belgian club side Kibubu after they pulled out for financial reasons. [1]
