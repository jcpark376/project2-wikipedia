Christelle Le Duff (born 21 November 1982) is a French female rugby union player. She represented France at the 2006 Women's Rugby World Cup, and 2014 Women's Rugby World Cup.[1] She was a member of France's 2013 Rugby World Cup Sevens squad.[2]
