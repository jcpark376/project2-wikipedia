Shahrdari Mahshahr Football Club is an Iranian football club based in Mahshahr, Iran. They currently compete in the Azadegan League.

History[edit]

Shahrdari Mashahr earned promotion to League 2 after finishing in first place of Group B in the 2014–15 edition of League 3. In 2017 for the first time in their history, the club was promoted to the 2nd tiered Azadegan League after finishing in first place of Group B.[1]
