Bovlstrup or Boulstrup is a village in Jutland, Denmark. It is located in Odder Municipality.

History[edit]

Bovlstrup Station was built in the village in 1883, as a stop on the Hads-Ning Hundreds' Railroad, connecting Bovlstrup to Hou, Odder and Aarhus. When the Hou-Odder stretch was shut down in 1977, the station in Bovlstrup was shut down as well.[2]
