Shane McGeown is a Northern Irish football Midfieldercurrently[when?] playing for Armagh City F.C. in the NIFL Championship.[1]

He was a member of the Armagh team that gained promotion to the Premier League in the 2004–05.

In the summer of 2006 McGeown left Armagh on a free Transfer to join Monaghan United F.C. in the League of Ireland.[1] He returned to Armagh in the January 2007 transfer window.
