Marche à l'ombre is a 1984 French comedy film written and directed by Michel Blanc.[2] The film was the most popular film in France in 1984[3] with over 6 million admissions.[1]

Cast[edit]

  • Gérard Lanvin - François
  • Michel Blanc - Denis
  • Sophie Duez - Mathilde
  • Katrine Boorman - Katrina
  • Mimi Félixine [fr] - Marie-Gabrielle
  • Béatrice Camurat [fr] - Martine
  • Maka Kotto - Joseph
  • Jean-François Dérec [fr] - Raymond
  • Bernard Farcy - Monsieur Christian
  • François Berléand - Police Inspector
