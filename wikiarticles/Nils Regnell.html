Nils Gustaf Regnell (18 December 1884 – 14 November 1950) was a Swedish freestyle swimmer. He competed at the 1906 Intercalated Games in the one mile and 4×250 m events and finished fifth in the relay. His younger sisters Elsa and Lisa were Olympic divers.[1][2]
