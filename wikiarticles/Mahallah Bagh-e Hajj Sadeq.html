Mahallah Bagh-e Hajj Sadeq (Persian: محله باغ حاج صادق‎, also Romanized as Maḩallah Bāgh-e Ḩājj Şādeq) is a village in Lajran Rural District, in the Central District of Garmsar County, Semnan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 7, in 6 families.[1]
