Hertleinia is a genus of sea snails, marine gastropod mollusks in the family Cancellariidae, the nutmeg snails.[1]

Species[edit]

Species within the genus Hertleinia include:

  • Hertleinia mitraformis (G.B. Sowerby I, 1832)[2]
