Bojmie [ˈbɔi̯mjɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kotuń, within Siedlce County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland.[1] It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) west of Kotuń, 22 km (14 mi) west of Siedlce, and 66 km (41 mi) east of Warsaw.
