'Mahanpur', is a town and a newly created tehsil in Kathua district in the union territory of Jammu and Kashmir, India. Earlier, Mahanpur was a town in Basohli Tehsil. It is located about 33 km North towards from District headquarters Kathua and 119 km from the union territory capital Jammu (winter) and 299 km from Srinagar (summer).

Geography[edit]

Mahanpur is located at 32°32′N 75°38′E﻿ / ﻿32.54°N 75.64°E﻿ / 32.54; 75.64,[2] It has an average elevation of 396 metres in the lower Sivalik Hills range of Himalaya. Mahanpur (Town) 919 has an area of 363 square kilometers.[3] Mahanpur borders Bani and Duggan Tehsil to the north, Billawar to the west and Dharkalan to the south.
