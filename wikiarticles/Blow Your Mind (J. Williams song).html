"Blow Your Mind" is the debut single from New Zealand R&B singer J. Williams. It features on his debut album, Young Love.[1] It was released digitally on 11 August 2008.[2] The single debuted on the RIANZ singles chart on 15 September 2008 at number 19. It later peaked at number 13.[3]
