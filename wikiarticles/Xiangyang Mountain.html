Xiangyang Mountain (Chinese: 向陽山; Hanyu Pinyin: Xiàngyáng Shān; Tongyong Pinyin: Siàngyáng Shan) is a mountain in Kaohsiung, Taiwan with an elevation of 3,603 m (11,821 ft).[1]
