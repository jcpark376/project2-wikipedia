Kizeh-ye Ajam (Persian: كيزه اجم‎, also Romanized as Kīzeh-ye Ājam) is a village in Ajam Rural District, Dishmok District, Kohgiluyeh County, Kohgiluyeh and Boyer-Ahmad Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[1]
