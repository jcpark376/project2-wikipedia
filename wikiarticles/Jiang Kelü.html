Jiang Kelü (Chinese: 蒋科律; pinyin: Jiǎng Kēlǜ; born 29 April 1985) is a Chinese sabre fencer. He competed in the men's team sabre competition at the 2012 Summer Olympics.[1] He is married to fellow Jiangsu fencer Zhu Min.[2]
