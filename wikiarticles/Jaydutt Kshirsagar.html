Jaidatta Kshirsagar is a leader of Shiv Sena from Beed district, Marathwada. He is past Member of Legislative Assembly from Beed (Vidhan Sabha constituency), and a former minister of Maharashtra.[2]

Positions held[edit]

  • 1990: Elected to Maharashtra Legislative Assembly [3]
  • 1999: Elected to Maharashtra Legislative Assembly [4]
  • 2009: Elected to Maharashtra Legislative Assembly
  • 2009: Appointed as Minister of Public Works Department in Maharashtra Government
  • 2014: Re-Elected to Maharashtra Legislative Assembly
  • 2019: Minister of Employment Guarantee and Horticulture in Maharashtra State Government [5]
