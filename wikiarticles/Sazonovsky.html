Sazonovsky (Russian: Сазоновский) is a rural locality (a khutor) in Rossoshinskoye Rural Settlement, Uryupinsky District, Volgograd Oblast, Russia. The population was 50 as of 2010.[2] There are 2 streets.

Geography[edit]

The village is located 13 km south-west from Uryupinsk.
