Kugul (Russian: Кугуль) is a rural locality (a village) in Tanovsky Selsoviet, Blagovarsky District, Bashkortostan, Russia. The population was 71 as of 2010.[2] There is 1 street.

Geography[edit]

It is located 18 km from Yazykovo, 3 km from Tan.
