Steve Noon is a British illustrator, born in Kent, England.

Since 1985, Noon has worked as a professional artist. Noon attended art college in Cornwall.[which?] His illustrations have been award winning, particularly those under publisher Dorling Kindersley.[citation needed]

Noon has illustrated books like A City Through Time and A Street Through Time[1] that are part of a collection of books.
