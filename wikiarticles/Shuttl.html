Shuttl is a mobile app based office commute bus aggregator based out of Gurgaon, India. The company was founded in 2015. It operates in more than 6 metro cities across the country.[2][3][4]

History[edit]

The company was founded by IITians Amit Singh and Deepanshu Malviya. Shuttl began intra-city bus fleet operations in 2015 within Delhi-NCR.[5]

Funding[edit]

Shuttl raised its first round of funding in 2015 and has since then attracted several marquee investors including Sequoia, Lightspeed, Times Internet, Amazon India, Toyota Tsusho among others.[2][6][7]

Awards[edit]

In June 2018, Shuttl was felicitated in the sustainable mobility category at the International Ashden Awards held in London.[8][9]

Earlier, in November 2017, in the FICCI Road Safety Awards, the firm had won the Special Jury Award in the category of Road Safety Product and Solution.[10][11]
