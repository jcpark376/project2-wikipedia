Deerfield in Nova Scotia

Deerfield (2006 population: 264) is a Canadian rural community located in Yarmouth County, Nova Scotia.

It is an administrative part of the Municipal District of Yarmouth, it is around 17 km from Yarmouth town and as the name suggests has many wild deer in the surrounding woods.
