Färila IF is a Swedish football club located in Färila.[1]

Background[edit]

Färila IF currently plays in Division 4 Hälsingland which is the sixth tier of Swedish football.[2] They play their home matches at the Färila IP in Färila.[3]

The club is affiliated to Hälsinglands Fotbollförbund.[4]
