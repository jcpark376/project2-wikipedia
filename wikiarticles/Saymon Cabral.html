Saymon de Barros Cabral (born 9 July 2001) is a Brazilian footballer who plays for Spartak Trnava, on loan from Avaí, as a right winger or a right back.

Club career[edit]

Saymon Cabral made his professional Fortuna Liga debut for Spartak Trnava against MŠK Žilina on 14 June 2020.[1]
