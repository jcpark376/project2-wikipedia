The genus Plecotus consists of the long-eared bats. Many species in the genus have only been described and recognized in recent years.[1]

Species[edit]

Genus Plecotus – long-eared bats

  • Plecotus ariel
  • Brown long-eared bat, Plecotus auritus
  • Grey long-eared bat, Plecotus austriacus
