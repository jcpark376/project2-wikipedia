Malthopsis is a genus of batfishes with a wide distribution in the world's oceans.

Species[edit]

There are currently 13 recognized species in this genus:

  • Malthopsis annulifera S. Tanaka (I), 1908
  • Malthopsis asperata H. C. Ho, C. D. Roberts & K. T. Shao, 2013 (Roughspine batfish)[1]
  • Malthopsis austrafricana H. C. Ho, 2013 (Southern African triangular batfish)[2]
  • Malthopsis bradburyae H. C. Ho, 2013 (Bradbury's triangular batfish) [2]
  • Malthopsis gigas H. C. Ho & K. T. Shao, 2010 (Giant triangular batfish)
  • Malthopsis gnoma Bradbury, 1998
  • Malthopsis jordani C. H. Gilbert, 1905
  • Malthopsis kobayashii S. Tanaka (I), 1916
  • Malthopsis lutea Alcock, 1891 (Longnose seabat)
  • Malthopsis mitrigera C. H. Gilbert & Cramer, 1897 (Twospine batfish)
  • Malthopsis parva H. C. Ho, C. D. Roberts & K. T. Shao, 2013 (Arrowhead batfish) [1]
  • Malthopsis retifera H. C. Ho, Prokofiev & K. T. Shao, 2009 (Reticulate triangular batfish)
  • Malthopsis tiarella D. S. Jordan, 1902 (Spearnose seabat)
