Claude Rich (8 February 1929 – 20 July 2017) was a French stage and screen actor.[1] He began his career in the theater before his film debut in 1955.

Personal life[edit]

This section does not cite any sources. Please help improve this section by adding citations to reliable sources. Unsourced material may be challenged and removed.
Find sources: "Claude Rich" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR (July 2017) (Learn how and when to remove this template message)

He married actress Catherine Renaudin on 26 June 1959. They had two daughters, Delphine (an actress) and Nathalie (a painter), and an adopted son, Remy.

Career[edit]

In 1996, he was a member of the jury at the 46th Berlin International Film Festival.[2]
