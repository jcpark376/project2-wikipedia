Kunashak

Rural locality in Chelyabinsk Oblast, Russia
Building in Kunashak

Kunashak (Russian: Кунашак) is a rural locality (a selo) and the administrative center of Kunashaksky District, Chelyabinsk Oblast, Russia. Population: 6,296 (2010 Census);[1] 5,993 (2002 Census);[2] 5,703 (1989 Census).[3]
