Mats is a given name, a Scandinavian form of Matthew or Matthias, and may refer to:

  • Mats Bergman, Swedish actor
  • Mats Gustafsson, Swedish saxophone player
  • Mats Hellström, Swedish politician
  • Mats Hinze
  • Mats Hummels, German footballer
  • Mats Johansson, Swedish journalist and politician
  • Mats Magnusson, former Swedish footballer
  • Mats Moraing, German tennis player
  • Mats Nilsson, Swedish grappler and mixed martial artist
  • Mats Nilsson, Swedish Air Force officer
  • Mats Öberg, Swedish pianist
  • Mats Odell, Swedish politician
  • Mats Paulson, Swedish singer and songwriter
  • Mats Persson
  • Mats Ronander, Swedish rock musician
  • Mats Sundin, former Swedish ice hockey player
  • Mats Wahl, Swedish author
  • Mats Wallberg, Swedish ice speed skater
  • Mats Waltin, ice hockey
  • Mats Wilander, former World No. 1 tennis player from Sweden
