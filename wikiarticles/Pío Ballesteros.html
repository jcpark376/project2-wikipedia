Pío Ballesteros (April 1, 1919 - 1995) was a Spanish writer, producer and director. He only directed three films: Consultaré a Mister Brown (1946), Facultad de Letras (1949) and El alma de la copla (1965).[1] José Luis López Vázquez worked with Ballesteros and Enrique Herreros as assistant director.[2]
