Kentucky Route 316 is a 7.755-mile state highway in Kentucky that runs from US 421 south of Bedford, Kentucky to KY 55 just north of Interstate 71.

Route description[edit]

KY 316 begins at US 421 south of Bedford. KY 316 winds north as Carlisle Road for 3 miles before intersecting with KY 1335. At KY 1335, KY 316 leaves Carlisle Road and turns to the southeast and travels about 41/2 miles, until it comes to an end at KY 55 just north of Interstate 71.

Major intersections[edit]

The entire route is in Trimble County.
