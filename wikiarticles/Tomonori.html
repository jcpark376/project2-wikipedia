Tomonori (written 智紀, 智徳, 智則, 友謙, 友則, 具教, 伴典) is a masculine Japanese given name. Notable people with the name include:

  • Tomonori Hirayama (平山 智規, born 1978), Japanese footballer
  • Tomonori Jinnai (陣内 智則, born 1974), Japanese comedian and television presenter
  • Ki no Tomonori (紀 友則, c. 850 – c. 904), Japanese poet
  • Kitabatake Tomonori (北畠 具教, 1528–1576), Japanese daimyō
  • Tomonori Kogawa (湖川 友謙, born 1950), Japanese animator and character designer
  • Tomonori Maeda (前田 智徳, born 1971), Japanese baseball player
  • Tomonori Ohara, Japanese mixed martial artist
  • Tomonori Tateishi (立石 智紀, born 1974), Japanese footballer
  • Tomonori Tsunematsu (恒松 伴典, born 1976), Japanese footballer

Fictional characters[edit]

  • Tomonori Michitani (蕗谷 トモノリ), a character in the anime series Super Doll Licca-chan
