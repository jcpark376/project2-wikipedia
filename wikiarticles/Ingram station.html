Ingram is a station on the Port Authority of Allegheny County West Busway located in Ingram, PA. It is served by:

  • 28X-Airport Flyer
  • G2-West Busway All Stops
  • G3-Moon Flyer
  • G31-Bridgeville Flyer

The station is the only station on the busway without a park and ride lot nearby.
