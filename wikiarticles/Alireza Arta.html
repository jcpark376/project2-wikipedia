Alireza Arta (Persian: علیرضا آرتا‎) (born February 4, 1997 in Kerman)[1] is an Iranian footballer who plays as defender for the Persian Gulf Pro League club Gol Gohar Sirjan.[2][3][4][5]

International career[edit]

U20[edit]

He is part of Iran U–23 during 2018 AFC U-23 Championship qualification.
