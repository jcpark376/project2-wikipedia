Ústrašice is a village and municipality (obec) in Tábor District in the South Bohemian Region of the Czech Republic.

The municipality covers an area of 7.4 square kilometres (2.9 sq mi), and has a population of 237 (as at 3 July 2006).

Ústrašice lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) south of Tábor, 44 km (27 mi) north of České Budějovice, and 86 km (53 mi) south of Prague.
