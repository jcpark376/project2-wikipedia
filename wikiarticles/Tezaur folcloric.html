Tezaur folcloric ((in English): Folk treasure) is a folk music TV show broadcast on TVR1 and TVR Moldova.[1] In 1983, the Romanian Television launched "Tezaur folcloric",[2] the show was made and presented, until 2014 by Marioara Murărescu.[3] Since 2014 the show is presented by Gheorghiţa Nicolae.[4] The theme music of the show is sung by Dumitru Fărcaș.[5][6]
