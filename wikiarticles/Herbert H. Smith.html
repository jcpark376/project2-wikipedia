Herbert H. Smith

Jump to navigation Jump to search
For others of the same name, see Herbert Smith (disambiguation).

Herbert H. Smith was a member of the Wisconsin State Assembly and the Wisconsin State Senate.

Biography[edit]

Smith was born on July 18, 1898 in Kingston, Ontario.[1] He attended South Division High School in Milwaukee, Wisconsin before graduating from the University of Wisconsin Law School in 1922.

Career[edit]

Smith was elected to the Assembly in 1924. Later, he represented the 7th district of the Senate from 1927 to 1930. He was a Republican.
