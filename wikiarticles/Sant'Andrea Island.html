Sant'Andrea Island

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search
For island in the Venetian Lagoon, see Sant'Andrea (Venetian Lagoon).
View towards the Island of Sant'Andrea.

Sant'Andrea Island (Italian: Isola di Sant'Andrea) is a small island near Gallipoli in the Ionian Sea.
